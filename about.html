Our Mission

EthioNews is committed to delivering accurate, timely, and comprehensive news coverage about Ethiopia and its diaspora communities. We strive to be the most trusted source of information for Ethiopians worldwide.

Our History

Founded in 2010, EthioNews began as a small online blog covering local news in Addis Ababa. Over the years, we've grown into Ethiopia's leading digital news platform, with correspondents across all regions and a global network of diaspora reporters.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced journalists, editors, and technologists dedicated to upholding the highest standards of journalism. We pride ourselves on our diverse team that represents all regions of Ethiopia.

Our Values

  • Accuracy: We verify all information before publication
  • Integrity: We maintain editorial independence
  • Inclusivity: We cover stories from all regions and communities
  • Innovation: We leverage technology to enhance storytelling
